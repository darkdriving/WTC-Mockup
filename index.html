WELCOME TO WHITETAIL CLUB

Whitetail Club is the premier private community in the Pacific Northwest. The allure of this hidden gem stems from the natural luxuries of the area, which are at once action-packed and serene. Seasons here present their own prospects for adventure and relaxation— from thrilling ski runs lauded by National Geographic to ancient hot springs. We invite you to experience luxury on the edge of the wild.
